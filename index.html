IEEE Student Branch College of Engineering Chengannur (60451) is organizing MVP - Micro Volunteering Programme for IEEE members, to focus on the development of volunteering skills, to enable networking and socialising with fellow members from the Student Branch. The program looks forward to build a trailblazing platform for the student leaders to interact across regions to share their knowledge, find their best applications, cultivate different ideas and much more.

Rules and Regulations

Micro Volunteering Programme (MVP) 2.0 is a one-month long event that provides an interactive learning experience for every participant, motivating them to keep striving to reach a goal.

  • To enroll in the volunteering program, the student must have a valid IEEE membership ID.
  • Online tasks will be provided to the volunteers on alternate days.
  • Reports of the completed task should be submitted via the Google Form that will be provided along with each task.
  • The successful completion of each task will reward you with points.
